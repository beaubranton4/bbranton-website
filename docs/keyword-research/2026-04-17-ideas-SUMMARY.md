**Date:** 2026-04-17  
**Source:** `/content/apple-notes/iCloud/Ideas` — p1800+ notes (most recent ~6-12 months)

## TL;DR

One monster find. One strong build-validate case. One large-but-contested cluster. Everything else is thin or owned.

**The monster:** "Cage batting near me" has 201,000 monthly searches at KD 0. The entire batting cages cluster — 28 keywords, every single one KD 0-14 — is transactional. Nobody ranks for it. A local sports facility directory would own this.

**The build-validate case:** "Summer camp for adult" is 6,600 vol / KD 0 / $2.57 CPC. Every city variant is also KD 0. This directly validates the RECESS concept from the journal research — people are searching for adult camp experiences and the SERP is empty.

**The large-but-contested cluster:** Amazon price tracking ("price history on amazon" — 18,100 vol, KD 28, $3.63 CPC). The cluster has 68 viable keywords and strong commercial intent. CamelCamelCamel exists but KD 28 means the SERP isn't locked. A browser extension or modern tool could compete.

**Kill list from ideas:** Vibe coding / Cursor AI — real volume (110k) but CI 45. Market owned by funded companies. Digital twin / personal AI — "ai memory app" has 10 searches/month; market isn't searching yet. Energy/caffeine gum — CI 72-100, Amazon dominated.
