## The marketing pain, specifically

The pain named on 07-17 — marketing material is hard to make, sharing what's built is a slog, growing a social channel and posting on Reddit is tiring — is genuine and highly pain-proximate. But it's not a fresh discovery: `solopreneur-landscape-2026-05-27.md` researched "distribution-as-a-system" as a pain category and concluded it's simultaneously the biggest demand *and* the worst graveyard, because the market is flooded with generic "AI growth tools" and that flood is itself the fatigue builders are reacting against. A scheduling/auto-poster tool for indie hackers also fails the ARPU rule outright — `dataset-business-2026-05-31.md` flagged that exact buyer as "$10-50/mo, cheap, high-churn."

The wedge that already passed the filter for this exact problem: **Bench Boss**, from `viral-product-brainstorm-2026-05-23.md` — an anonymous AI-manager character account that generates shareable content about Dugout Edge as it's being built. Scored as the cleanest, lowest-infrastructure-cost idea of 17 candidates specifically *because* it's a marketing layer bolted onto an existing roadmap, not a new venture with its own distribution problem. If Dugout Edge is ever fully off the table, the generalized version of that pattern — a character-led AI agent that turns a builder's raw progress into shareable content — is a sharper, non-generic version of "solve my own marketing pain" than a generic growth-tool SaaS would be.
