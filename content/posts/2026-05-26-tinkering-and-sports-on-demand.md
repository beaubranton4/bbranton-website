May 26, 2026

I like to tinker and I like to play with things. My interest wanes quickly, but when I get obsessed with something I can focus for hours on end, days and weeks at a time.

I really wish there was a way that I could play sports — fun, interactive, and competitive team sports like soccer, or basketball, or baseball — but without other people. Not like video games. Truly playing in the physical world, but on command and at any given time. Without having to worry about getting 17 other people to meet at a specific time and place; without having to worry about others getting their feelings hurt about playing time; without worrying about the competition level being too easy or too hard.
