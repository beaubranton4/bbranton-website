June 16, 2026

Bad habits compound, and I have been letting my bad habits compound over the last couple of weeks. One thing after the other, falling back into the old patterns. It doesn't hit me immediately, but I know that over time it's just going to compound and I'm going to slowly fall backwards and be stuck exactly where I am — having time pass without me feeling like I've made any progress toward my goals, having not enjoyed being in the moment, simply coasting and walking through life like a zombie.

I have made some good progress on Dugout Edge — or at least my mind is telling me that I have — but in reality I need to put things out into the world. I need to quit falling into this pattern of developing over month-to-month cycles. I need to push fast now that I have the power of AI, and I need to just get it out there into the world, engage the interest, get feedback.

I think for me the hardest thing is being comfortable with delivering an unfinished product to my true customers. But I need to think of everything like an A/B test and move fast. Nobody's going to churn because there's a new feature that sucks, right? So I just need to get it out there, get feedback, get it out there, get feedback.

So I really need to make it a goal to myself to launch the GameDay tool tomorrow. By Wednesday. Shoot — maybe just launch it by today. That should be my goal. The advanced stat tracking is going to be difficult to launch today, but make it a goal to launch tomorrow.

Other than that, I feel like I've been working out a good amount, but I've been eating a lot too. Can't get those calories back that you over-consume. Over-consuming a tub of ice cream, a bag of chips — that's three workouts during the week, and you're never going to overcome that. But I've been on training schedule. I've been running a little bit more, feeling a lot healthier cardiovascular-wise. My body feels a little bit stronger when I get into good running form, which I do really like. But I don't see the changes necessarily in my body, and I still feel like the body fat is there. Really wish I would have measured myself at the marathon — see what percent body fat I was at.

Had some dreams about playing baseball. Getting put into a starting lineup, hitting a home run. Gosh, it was such a big dream. I really do miss playing baseball. Such a fun sport.

I really do think I need to just take on Dugout, take on GameChanger. Might sound silly, but why not? I can go for it.

And I need to learn to copy things more. Don't be afraid to copy. There's nothing wrong — absolutely nothing wrong — with copying. There's a reason why people did what they did. Instead of spending the time to figure it out myself, let others do the work for me.
