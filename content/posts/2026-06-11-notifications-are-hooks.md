June 11, 2026

I just heard the most beautiful phrase. Beautiful might be an overstatement. It's just very, very thought provoking and very, very insightful, but also very obvious.

Notifications are what other people want you to do on your phone.

Yet, every time your phone rings, every time it buzzes, every time you get a notification, it's addicting. You take 10 seconds of your time to check it. You often click into it, whether it's LinkedIn, whether it's Instagram, whether it's Twitter, whether... It's just snapping at your attention, trying to get you to bite.

It's like a hook and bait in the water and you're a fish. It's just notification, notification, notification. Each notification is a hook that's trying to lure you out of the water. And it's honestly debilitating.

It's distracting.
