May 11, 2026

Today was a pretty good day. Had a nice talk with mom in the kitchen in between meetings.

Worked up the courage to bring up Alzheimer's, and expressed that I felt she should speak to her doctor about getting some cognitive testing. It's hard to accept the fact that your parents are aging. But all I can do is be present and be happy that I have her — and all of her — now.

Work has been picking up and I'm not sure if I love that.

I want to remember the conversation I had with my mom this morning about taking the leap, and how she basically gave me the permission to do so. How we talked about it's not really a risk and that I should just go for it. But that I should probably meet people, talk with people who have similar interests.
