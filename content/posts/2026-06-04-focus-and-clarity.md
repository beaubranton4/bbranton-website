June 4, 2026

I need to spend more time focusing on focus and clarity. Often I have way too many things that I'm trying to do at one given time. And I just need to narrow it down to my most important priorities.

So for example, when I'm working on Dugout Edge, of course there's a long to-do list of things that I could do. That list is going to be 30 items, 40 items, 50 items long. But what I need to do on a daily basis is pick one thing — just one thing — and finish it. Once I finish that, then I can assess, okay, what else can I do? But otherwise, I'm going to look at these 10 things, get distracted, start working on another thing, start working on another thing.

It's kind of why Dugout Edge has so many different free tools that are just like 70% built out. In some ways I think that that is actually really important. It's a good way to put feelers out, test demand. But on the other hand, I'm not investing my time in actions or things that actually move the needle for the business.

I need to remember that Dugout Edge saves time for coaches who manage baseball and softball teams. That's the crux of it. Anything else is wasted effort.
