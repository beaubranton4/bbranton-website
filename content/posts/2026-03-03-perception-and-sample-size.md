March 3, 2026

Something Dugout Edge is teaching me: just because someone says it is an issue—it doesn't mean it is an issue. And it certainly doesn't make it any more of an issue now than it was before they told you. It's all about perception.

We heavily weight the things we observe. We read something on Twitter, so it suddenly becomes more true than before we read it. That is not the fact. The fact is that you have some evidence, potentially a data point that can lead us in that direction. But we shouldn't overindex on a single data point. We need a larger sample size.

Also, it always tends to be the non-paying users that ask for the most. Be careful who you help and who you don't when running your own business and doing your own customer service.
